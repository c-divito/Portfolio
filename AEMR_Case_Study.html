AEMR Case Study

The objective of the AEMR (America's Energy Market Regulator) Case Study is to analyze and understand the various aspects of forced outages in the energy sector during the years 2016-2017.

~2,900 outages were unintended in 2016-2017

One of the biggest concerns are forced outages since they happen without notice. To fix this, there should be measures implemented to reduce that number.
