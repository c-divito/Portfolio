Decoding Youtube Trends

Finding key factors that contributed to the success of YouTube videos from July 2006 to June 2008. By discerning trends in video titles, tags, content types and dates, actionable recommendations for our new Youtuber will be issued to implement in their content strategy. The success was determined based on number of views.

Music was the Dominating Category

Views were positively correlated with Likes and Comment Count

When views goes up, so do likes and comments.

Videos Published on Fridays have significantly more Views, Comments and Likes

Titles Including the Words "Official" and "Video" Increase Viewership

Including the Tags "Funny" and/or "How to"
