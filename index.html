Southern Water Corporation

Southern Water Corporation is selling fresh water from Surjek, Kootha and Jutik but the demand for it is rising along with its revenues. Southern Water Corp. needs to maximize the availability of its three desalination plants without an unsustainable rise in costs.

ChemCorp Inc.

ChemCorp Inc., a leading chemical producer, has experienced consistent growth of 7% Year-on-Year. However, increased competition has led to a 10% decline in sales revenue from customers. To safeguard the customer-base and ensure the company's ability to make dividend payments to shareholders, ChemCorp aims to mitigate this issue. The objective is to achieve a 7% increase in revenue by December 31st, 2023.
